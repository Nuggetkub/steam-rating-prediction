── ENCODING IMPROVEMENTS ───────────────────────────────────────────────

7. Bayesian smoothing for mean target encoding
   - Current: unknown developer/publisher falls back to global mean (7.97)
   - Problem: developers with only 1 game get a perfect mean = 10 or 0
   - Fix: smoothed_mean = (count * mean + m * global_mean) / (count + m)
     where m ~ 5-10 (shrinks small-sample devs toward global mean)
   - Reduces over-reliance on developer_rating_mean for new studios
   - TESTED (m=1, m=5, pre-split): WORSE — smoothing compressed the range and diluted
     the developer signal. Full means are better for this dataset.
   - TESTED (weight=15, post-split): CATASTROPHIC — dev range [1.06-10.00]→[6.47-8.75], baseline RMSE 0.63→1.20
   - TESTED (weight=3, post-split, First-Time Dev grouping): WORSE — test RMSE 0.6108→0.6851, hold-out R²=−0.03;
     8,472 devs (48%) collapsed to ftd_mean≈gm; developer_rating_mean importance 38%→5%; CV/test gap +0.063
   - ROOT CAUSE (permanent): 44% of devs have exactly 1 game. Any post-split or smoothed approach
     collapses nearly half the dev population to global_mean, destroying the #1 feature.
   - NOT APPLICABLE for this dataset structure.

8. K-fold mean target encoding
   - Current approach computes means on full df_clean (minor train/test leakage)
   - Proper fix: use category_encoders.TargetEncoder with cv=5
   - Prevents the model from seeing the target signal for its own folds
   - TESTED: CATASTROPHICALLY WORSE (RMSE 0.6339→1.1208, R² 0.81→0.41)
   - Root cause: 9,639 developers (44%) have exactly 1 game. OOF encoding
     collapses all of them to the global mean, destroying the developer signal.
   - NOT APPLICABLE for this dataset structure.

9. Publisher/developer career stats beyond mean
   - Add: developer_game_count (how many games they have)
   - Add: developer_rating_std (consistency of quality)
   - Add: developer_last_game_rating (most recent release performance)
   - TESTED: ✅ KEPT — test RMSE 0.6336->0.6120 (best ever); developer_last_rating rank 3, std rank 13, game_count not in top 20
